Vantage ▸
Certainauto-success — directly stated or implied
Reasonable+0/1t — fits the PC & situation
Doubtful+2t or more — possible but unlikely
Impossibleno roll — beyond vantage
Vantage = everything about the PC (backgrounds, talents, past) plus setting & situation. Rolls without risk are auto-successes.

Pools ▸
Roll the pool, drop every die showing 1–3. At 0d the event happens. Nothing dropped → PC may push (mark, drop 1d) or pivot (secondary effect), or the GM spends suspense to drop 1d.

Conditions & Healing ▸
Specific maladies; +1t when they hinder a roll. Often a diminishing pool timer.
Urgenta timer toward a worse fate
Short-termwears off shortly
Long-termneeds treatment or lots of time
Permanentsubstantial intervention; player buy-in
Healing: treatment carries risk and needs time/tools/training (potency can waive these). Marks are too minor to heal; harm needs treatment; conditions heal when it makes sense.
