Stats & Harm

Mark a stat: +1t on its next roll, then clear. If it's already marked, take harm instead. Bloodied (physical) +1t Brawn/Agility · Rattled (mental) +1t Wits/Presence · Desperate +1t all. A 2nd desperate drops you. Tap a stat's Roll to throw it with its current thorns loaded.

Conditions free-form pools

Examples: daunted · strained · unsteady · winded · battered · confused · 4d poisoned · 8d witch's curse. Give it a die pool if it's a timer (e.g. 4d confused) — roll it to drop 1–3s. +1t when a condition hinders a roll.
